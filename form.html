Bike Suitability Calculator



This calculator uses various metrics about a road segment to determine the suitability of that road segment for bikes and pedestrians. The various metrics can be found at the bottom of the page, with links to more information. If you would like to submit the data and scores for your input, you can press the submit button at the bottom of the page to store it in a central database. If you encounter a bug, you can submit a bug report on our GitHub page.

Rating Scale

5: Perfect or near perfect pavement

4: Occasional bumps and cracks, still good to ride on

3: Pavement in need of some upkeep, potholes exist

2: Pavement needs upkeep, has lots of potholes and discontiuities
